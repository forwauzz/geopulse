# Distribution Formats

## Article

- canonical site asset
- deeper explanation
- should satisfy blog LLM-readiness rules
- should usually contain:
  - title
  - opening definition/problem framing
  - answer blocks
  - concrete sections
  - restrained CTA

## Newsletter

- downstream distribution asset
- shorter than the article
- preserves the same argument
- more scannable
- may point back to the canonical article or free scan

## Brief

- not publishable content
- planning artifact that locks:
  - audience
  - angle
  - claims
  - proof boundary
  - CTA
