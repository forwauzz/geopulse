# Article Brief

## Asset
- type: article
- target persona: SEO consultants
- secondary persona: agency owners
- working title: How to Audit Your Site for AI Search Readiness
- topic cluster: ai_search_readiness

## Problem
- core problem: many teams still do not know how to tell whether their site is actually understandable to AI search systems
- why it matters now: teams can rank in traditional search and still be hard for AI systems to crawl, extract from, or trust

## GEO-Pulse angle
- what GEO-Pulse can credibly say: GEO-Pulse can frame a practical readiness audit around crawl access, extractability, structure, internal linking, and trust signals without pretending to predict rankings
- what GEO-Pulse must not claim: do not promise citations, rankings, traffic lifts, or benchmark-backed win rates the product does not currently support

## Key points
- a useful AI-search-readiness audit starts with structure, not hype
- crawlable does not automatically mean extractable
- schema helps, but readability, trust, and page structure still matter
- teams need a practical checklist, not another vague GEO manifesto
- the right CTA is to run a free scan, not to sell hard inside the article
