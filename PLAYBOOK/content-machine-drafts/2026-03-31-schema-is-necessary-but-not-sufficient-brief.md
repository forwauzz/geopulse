## Problem

## Key points
- schema is useful confirmation, not a substitute for clear page structure
- visible content and structured data need to agree
- weak pages are not rescued by correct markup alone
- schema should support a page that is already understandable
- the right CTA is to run a free scan to check structure and machine-readable basics together
