9.	Quantitative Density: Count of hard numbers, dates, and units (LLMs cite "Specifics" over "Vagueness").
10.	Schema Graph Depth: Use of FAQPage, TechArticle, Dataset, and Person schema to link entities.
11.	Standalone Context Score: Can a single paragraph be understood if removed from the page?

12.	Entity Density: Number of recognized "Wikidata" entities per 100 words.
13.	Semantic Density: Coherence of the topic (does the content wander or stay on-topic?).
14.	Factual Consensus Score: How well do the site's facts align with the "Internet Consensus" for that topic?
15.	Inference Advantage: How many "steps of logic" must an LLM take to find an answer? (Low is better).
16.	Sentiment Bias: Is the tone objective/technical or marketing/superlative?
17.	Topical Breadth: Does the site cover the "Semantic Neighborhood" of its core topic?
18.	Vector Proximity: Cosine similarity of the site's embeddings to "High-Authority" sources (like Wikipedia/NYT).
19.	Citation Recency: When was the last time this specific "fact" was updated?
